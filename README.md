# 🍕 Bindass Cafe — Official Website

> **"Food, fun, and unforgettable moments — only at Bindass Cafe."**

A beautiful, fully animated 3D scrolling website for **Bindass Cafe**, Mathura's favourite fast food & street food hangout near the ATV Project.

---

## ✨ Features

- **3D Scroll Animations** — Elements reveal with depth, parallax, and perspective transforms as you scroll
- **Mouse-Tracked Cursor Glow** — Soft saffron radial glow follows your cursor across the page
- **3D Tilt Cards** — Menu cards and the About card respond to mouse movement with live perspective rotation
- **Floating Food Parallax** — Emoji food items float and drift at different depths while scrolling
- **Marquee Ticker** — Seamless infinite-scroll menu name strip
- **Counter Animations** — Stats (rating, price, etc.) count up when they enter the viewport
- **Scroll-Reveal System** — Fade up, slide left, slide right — staggered with delay classes
- **Sticky Nav** — Transparent on load, frosted glass blur effect on scroll
- **Instagram Section** — 12-cell visual grid linking to `@bindass_cafe4`
- **Specials Horizontal Scroll** — Touch-friendly drag-scroll card strip for crowd favourites
- **Ratings Block** — Justdial (4.9), Magicpin (4.9), and Zomato (3.6) displayed side by side
- **Fully Responsive** — Adapts cleanly to mobile, tablet, and desktop
