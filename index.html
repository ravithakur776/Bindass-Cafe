Our Story

THE BINDASS VIBE

Bindass Cafe is a lively Mathura hangout near the ATV Project, serving a wide mix of fast food, pizzas, burgers, momos, Chinese, noodles, thalis, biryani, bakery items, and cold coffee.

We keep a fun, casual food-first vibe. Whether you're craving street-style momos, a loaded pizza, a Momo Burger, or a refreshing cold coffee — Bindass has you covered.

🛵 Home Delivery 🥡 Takeaway 🥦 Pure Veg 🍽️ Lunch & Dinner 📍 Near ATV Project
